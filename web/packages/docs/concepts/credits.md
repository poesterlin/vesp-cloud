# Credits & Pricing

VESP Cloud uses a pay-per-build model. You buy credit packs to cover firmware
builds. There is no subscription.

## How credits work

- **1 credit = 1 cloud firmware build.**
- Credits are deducted when you start a build. If the build fails due to a
  validation error before compilation begins, fix the errors and build again
  — no extra credit is charged for validation failures before compilation.
- Credits never expire.
- Credits are non-transferable and have no cash value.

## Credit packs

| Pack | Credits | Price (EUR) | Cost per build |
|------|---------|-------------|----------------|
| **Starter** | 10 | &euro;5.00 | &euro;0.50 |
| **Builder** | 50 | &euro;20.00 | &euro;0.40 |
| **Pro** | 200 | &euro;60.00 | &euro;0.30 |

The Builder pack is recommended for most users.

## Free self-hosting

You do **not** need credits to use VESP Cloud. You can export the complete
generated ESPHome code (YAML + C++) and compile and flash it yourself with your
own ESPHome setup. This costs nothing.

From the deploy page, choose **Download code** to get a ZIP file containing
everything you need to build and flash the firmware locally.

## Buying credits

1. Open the [Credits page](https://app.vesp.cloud/credits) or click the
   **Credits** link in the editor toolbar.
2. Select a credit pack.
3. You will be asked to consent to immediate delivery of digital build credits.
   This is required under EU consumer law because digital services begin
   immediately after purchase.
4. Complete payment through Stripe. Credits are added to your balance
   automatically.

## Right of withdrawal (EU only)

If you are an EU consumer, you have a 14-day right of withdrawal for credit
purchases. However, if you use credits to run a build within those 14 days, you
lose the right of withdrawal because the digital service has been fully
performed.

To submit a withdrawal request, use the [withdrawal form](https://app.vesp.cloud/withdrawal).
You will need your Stripe order ID (starts with `cs_`).

## Account deletion

If you delete your account, unused credits are forfeited and cannot be recovered.
See the [Terms of Service](https://app.vesp.cloud/terms) for full details.
